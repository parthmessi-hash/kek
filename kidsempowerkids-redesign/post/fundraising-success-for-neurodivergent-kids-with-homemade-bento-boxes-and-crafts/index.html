Fundraising Success for Neurodivergent Kids with Homemade Bento Boxes and Crafts

kidsempowerkidsfun Oct 1, 2024 2 min read

At Kids Empower Kids, we believe in creating meaningful change, and once again, we set out to raise funds to support neurodivergent children in our community. This time, we took a fresh approach, combining homemade meals and handmade crafts to bring together flavors and creativity for a worthy cause.

Bento Boxes: A Delicious Treat for a Good Cause

Our signature bento boxes were a hit! Each box was filled with homemade dishes, crafted with love and care:

Cauliflower and Potato: A warm, savory blend of spices that brought a comforting taste of home.

Garbanzo Beans: Packed with flavor and protein, this healthy dish was a fan favorite.

Lentil and Rice: A classic, hearty dish that offered nourishment and deliciousness in every bite.

Our team worked hard to prepare these meals from scratch, making sure each bento box was a perfect balance of flavor and nutrition. People loved the idea of grabbing a wholesome, homemade meal while contributing to a great cause.

Handmade Crafts: A Creative Touch

Alongside the bento boxes, we also featured an array of handmade crafts:

Headbands and Earrings: Beautifully crafted accessories, each one unique, made by us

Keychains and Bracelets: Fun, colorful, and perfect for gifts or a personal keepsake.

These handmade items added a personal touch to the fundraiser, allowing people to walk away not only with a delicious meal but also with a piece of art they could cherish.

Coming Together for a Greater Purpose

This event was more than just a fundraiser—it was an opportunity for the community to come together, share a meal, and support a cause close to our hearts. The funds raised will directly benefit neurodivergent children, providing them with resources and opportunities that will empower them to thrive.

We are incredibly grateful to everyone who contributed, whether through buying a bento box or a handcrafted item. Your support helps us continue our mission to uplift and empower children who need it the most.

Looking Ahead

As we reflect on the success of this event, we are excited to continue finding new, creative ways to raise funds for important causes. Stay tuned for our next fundraiser as we keep working to make a difference, one meal and one handmade item at a time!

Thank you for being a part of our journey to support neurodivergent kids. Together, we are making a lasting impact!
