Summer Fundraising Success – "Made with Love" for Neurodivergent Kids

kidsempowerkidsfun Sep 29, 2024 1 min read

This summer, we wanted to keep up the momentum and maximize our fundraising efforts for neurodivergent kids, so we continued with our "Made with Love" fundraiser. Once again, we dedicated all funds raised to support this important cause.

For this fundraiser, we decided to hold a sale of handcrafted jewelry, plants, bracelets, and Indian pickles. Unlike other events we’ve hosted, this one required a significant amount of prep work. We had to carefully create unique jewelry designs that would appeal to everyone and thoughtfully select the plants that would be most popular. After some research, we chose holy basil, beans, mint, and aloe vera—and we were fortunate to have several generous donors contribute these plants, which really helped make the sale a success.

As for the Indian pickles, we decided to feature three classic options: carrot pickle, mixed veggie pickle, and mango pickle. These flavors offered a taste of home and were well-received by our supporters.

All in all, the "Made with Love" fundraiser was a success. Not only did we raise money for neurodivergent kids, but we also brought the community together in a meaningful way. Seeing everyone come out to support this cause was heartwarming and a reminder of how much can be achieved when people unite for a common goal.

We’re incredibly grateful to everyone who contributed their time, resources, and creativity to make this event possible, and we’re excited to carry this positive energy into our future efforts!
