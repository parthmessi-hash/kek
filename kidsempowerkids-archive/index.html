Youth-Led Nonprofit

KIDS EMPOWER KIDS

Founded in 2021, Kids Empower Kids is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit created from the vision and passion of children who believed they could make a real difference in the world. To date, the team has raised over $35,000 to support organizations providing essential resources to families and communities in need. This youth-led movement continues to prove that compassion and leadership have no age limit.

When kids unite for a cause, they can achieve incredible things. Join our team of passionate volunteers as we spread awareness, organize events, and work to uplift those who need it most—because your time and heart can truly change lives.
